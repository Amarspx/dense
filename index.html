Dense is a jQuery plugin that offers an easy way to serve device pixel ratio aware images, bringing in retina support to your website. Clear the blurry, images be crisp.

Dense as the demoman

Your device pixel ratio was seen as and rounded up to . As such, you are seeing the x variation of the above image. The image tag doesn't define data-1x attribute, so we are defaulting to the src.

This responsive image is implemented by simply including the plugin on the page and calling its $.fn.dense() initializer method.

<script src="jquery.min.js"></script>
<script src="dense.min.js"></script>
<script>
    $('img').dense();
</script>
